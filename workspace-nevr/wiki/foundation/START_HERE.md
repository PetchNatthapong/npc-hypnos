# 🚀 Phase 1: Foundation — START HERE

## Welcome to Neurology

Neurology is the study of the nervous system — the most complex system in the human body. This phase builds the structural foundation you need before tackling clinical neurology.

---

```

│

│   └── Lobes, sulci, gyri, deep nuclei, ventricles, blood supply
```

---

## 🎯 Why This Order?

> **Structure before function.** You need to know *where* things are before you can understand *what* they do.
